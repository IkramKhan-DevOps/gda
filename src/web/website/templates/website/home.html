Safe Environment

We are dedicated to cleaning Galiyat to create a healthier and more inviting environment. Additionally, our efficient snow operations ensure that roads and pathways are cleared promptly, enhancing safety and accessibility for tourists. These efforts not only improve the quality of life for local residents but also attract more visitors, boosting the local economy. By maintaining cleanliness and timely snow removal, we ensure that Galiyat remains a pristine and welcoming destination year-round.

Building a Greener Future

Saving Trees

We actively promote tree planting and raise awareness about the harms of deforestation by organizing various campaigns for locals and educating both tourists and residents on the importance of planting trees. Additionally, we take responsibility for nurturing these trees, recognizing their crucial role in maintaining a healthy environment.

Ensuring Safety

At Galiyat, we prioritize every aspect of safety by launching snow operations promptly as soon as it starts snowing. We conduct rescue missions and clear landslides while continuously monitoring all activities. Our team works closely with local and traffic police to ensure a safe environment for families.

Fresh Environment

The Galiyat Forest is home to a diverse range of plant species, wildlife, trees, and herbs. Visit us to breathe the fresh air and experience the natural beauty of this gem, the number one tourist destination in Pakistan.
